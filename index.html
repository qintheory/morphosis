morphosis is a nature-inspired growing system which recycles 100% of food waste to produce biofertilizer. It is the hybrid of ancient technology, anaerobic digestion, and modern agritech, hydroponics enable growing new crops locally and economically without chemical additions.

How it works

morphosis uses anaerobic digestion to ferment organic waste while perserving almost 100% of the nutrients, i.e. N, P, K, and minerals. This produces both a methane-rich gas that can be used as a fuel to power the system, and a also biofertiliser that can be used in farming to boost crop growth.
